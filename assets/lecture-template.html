Overview: Ethical Evidence-Based Decision Making

Overview

Every day, we make many decisions. Some are simple, while others involve uncertainty, ethics, and risk.

This course examines how evidence and ethical reasoning can be integrated into structured decision-making.

📘

Key Concept

EEBDM combines evidence, ethics, and contextual judgment into a structured reasoning framework.
